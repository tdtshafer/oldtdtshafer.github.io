Personal Life

Travis lives off-campus with his beautiful wife, Jenna Marie Shafer. He spends time operating his commercial photography business, making music, playing sports, and doing dishes. He also operates a social media account for social justice called @ForgetPorn, encouraging men to escape the dangerous addiction of pornography. Travis wants to serve people the way that Jesus did, and volunteers with groups like The Bay and I've Got a Name in Lincoln. He is currently a senior at UNL, writing his Honors Thesis about human trafficking and sexual exploitation in urban and rural Nebraska and Thailand.
